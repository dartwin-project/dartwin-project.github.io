# Postdoctoral Researcher Position

## Position Title
**University Assistant / Postdoctoral Researcher (40h, 2 years)**

## Organizational Unit
DarTwin Project - Johannes Kepler University (JKU) Linz, Austria

## Job Duties

We are seeking a highly motivated postdoctoral researcher to join the DarTwin project at Johannes Kepler University Linz. The successful candidate will:

- Conduct autonomous research within the framework of the DarTwin project
 - Participate in joint research projects focused on Digital Twins, model-driven engineering, simulation, and software modelling
 - Collaborate with international partners at the University of Antwerp
 - Publish and present scientific results in journals and at international conferences
 - Supervise Bachelor's and Master's theses
 - Develop software tools, modelling workflows, and simulation pipelines relevant to Digital Twins

## Qualifications

- **Required**: Doctorate/Ph.D. degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Systems Engineering, Computational Modelling, or a closely related field
- Knowledge of model-driven engineering, digital twins, software modelling, simulation, or data-driven methods is an advantage
- Experience with software development (Python, Java, or similar), modelling languages/tools or simulation frameworks is highly desirable
- Very good command of English (spoken and written) (C1 level)
- Knowledge of German is an advantage
- Ability to work independently and as part of an international team
- Strong publication record or demonstrable software/tool contributions

## What We Offer

- A dynamic and international research environment
- Access to computing resources and collaboration with modelling and software research groups
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement
- Competitive salary according to the collective agreement (minimum €4,752.30 gross/month, 14 times per year)
- Full-time position (40 hours/week) for 2 years
- Collaboration with leading research groups in digital modelling and software engineering

## Application Process

If you are interested in this position, please send an email with the subject line **"Application Postdoc - DarTwin"** to [stefan.klikovits@jku.at](mailto:stefan.klikovits@jku.at).

Please include:
- A brief statement of interest
- Your CV, including information about your undergraduate and graduate grades
- List of publications
- Contact information for 2-3 references

**Note**: No need for a separate cover letter or certificates at the initial stage.

## Additional Information

This position is part of the DarTwin project, a collaborative research initiative between Johannes Kepler University Linz and the University of Antwerp. 

The position is available immediately and will remain open until filled. We especially welcome postdocs with fellowships and are happy to support fellowship applications.

For more information about our research, please visit our [research page]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/research/) or contact us directly.

We actively foster a diverse, inclusive, and respectful workplace. We warmly welcome applications from women, ethnic minorities, persons with disabilities, and people of all sexual orientations and gender identities.
