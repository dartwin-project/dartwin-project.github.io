# Postdoctoral Researcher Position

## Position Title
**University Assistant / Postdoctoral Researcher**

## Organizational Unit
DarTwin Project - University of Antwerp, Belgium

## Job Duties

We are seeking a highly motivated postdoctoral researcher to join the DarTwin project at the University of Antwerp. The successful candidate will:

- Conduct autonomous research within the framework of the DarTwin project
- Participate in joint research projects focused on Digital Twins, model-driven engineering, simulation, and data-driven modelling
- Collaborate with international partners at JKU Linz
- Publish and present scientific results in journals and at international conferences
- Supervise Bachelor's and Master's theses (if applicable)
- Develop software tools, simulation workflows, and modelling methods relevant to Digital Twins

## Qualifications

- **Required**: Doctorate/Ph.D. degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Systems Engineering, Computational Modelling, or a closely related field
- Strong background in model-driven engineering, digital twins, software modelling, simulation, or data-driven methods
- Experience with software development (Python, Java, or similar), modelling languages (e.g., SysML/UML) or simulation tools is highly desirable
- Excellent command of English (spoken and written)
- Ability to work independently and as part of an international team
- Strong publication record or demonstrable software/tool contributions

## What We Offer

- A dynamic and international research environment
- Access to computing resources and collaboration with modelling and software engineering research groups
- Opportunities for professional development and career advancement
- Competitive salary and benefits package
- Collaboration with leading research groups in digital modelling and software engineering

## Application Process

If you are interested in this position, please send an email with the subject line **"Application Postdoc - DarTwin"** to [joachim.denil@uantwerpen.be](mailto:joachim.denil@uantwerpen.be).

Please include:
- A brief statement of interest
- Your CV, including information about your undergraduate and graduate grades
- List of publications
- Contact information for 2-3 references

**Note**: No need for a separate cover letter or certificates at the initial stage.

## Additional Information

This position is part of the DarTwin project, a collaborative research initiative between the University of Antwerp and Johannes Kepler University Linz. The project focuses on advancing our understanding of quantum materials through innovative experimental approaches.

For more information about our research, please visit our [research page]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/research/) or contact us directly.
